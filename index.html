About Khoj

As Khoj celebrates its 13th anniversary, it's like looking back on a cherished childhood friend who has grown into a remarkable adult. Over the years, this interschool competition has nurtured young minds, sparking creativity and critical thinking.
This year, Khoj invites us on a journey into the future. Imagine India in 2047, 100 years after gaining independence. What will our nation look like? How will we have changed? This theme encourages students to dream big, to envision a future where technology, society, and governance have evolved.

Frequently Asked Questions

KHOJ is a commemorative event of talents, creativity and dedication of the young minds of the student community. It offers students from various schools a unique platform for the exchange of ideas and the cultivation of skills.

KHOJ invites participation from students across schools, offering a platform for innovators, thinkers, and creators to showcase their talents and ideas. This event is open to all students from Pre-Primary to Class XII.

The theme for Khoj #13.0 is "Envisioning India 2047," encouraging students to dream big and explore the future of technology, society, and governance.

Participants can register for Khoj by visiting the official Khoj website and filling out the registration form. Make sure to follow the guidelines provided.
